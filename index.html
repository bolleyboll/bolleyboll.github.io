Hydrocarbon Exploration using Seismic Imaging

July 2018 - April 2019

Seismic-data interpretation has as it’s main goal the identification of compartments, faults, fault sealing, and trapping mechanism that hold hydrocarbons. Our solution to the problem is to use U-Nets. Each pixel in the image is checked for the presence of salt and further on this is how the proportion of salt in that seismic image is calculated. Our model boasts an accuracy of 94.7%.
